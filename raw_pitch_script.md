### **The Takeer 2-Minute Demo Script**

**0:00 - 0:20 | The Hook (Visual: Quick cuts of Instagram/WhatsApp business pages)**

> "In Tanzania, social commerce is exploding. Thousands of merchants sell on Instagram and WhatsApp every day. But there’s a massive 'Trust Gap.' Buyers are afraid to pay upfront because of 'scams,' and merchants are tired of managing manual delivery logistics through random bodas and bus drivers. This friction is killing millions of shillings in potential sales."

**0:20 - 0:50 | The Product (Visual: Screen recording of the Takeer 1-click checkout)**

> "Meet **Takeer**. We aren't just another marketplace; we are the trust infrastructure.
> A merchant simply drops their Takeer link in their bio. When a buyer clicks, they don't see a complex form. They see a high-speed, 1-click checkout. Our system silently captures their GPS location and calculates real-time shipping fees for any zone in Dar or upcountry. One tap, one USSD push, and the payment is secured."

**0:50 - 1:20 | The "Magic" (Visual: Screen recording of the Merchant App scanning a waybill)**

> "But here is where the magic happens. We’ve automated the 'Kariakoo-to-Upcountry' nightmare.
> When a merchant sends a package via a bus, they simply snap a photo of the waybill. Our background AI extracts the tracking number and instantly pays the local boda driver. Most importantly, the funds stay in **Escrow**. They are only released to the merchant when the buyer confirms receipt at the terminal—or after our 72-hour 'Proof-of-Delivery' timer expires."

**1:20 - 1:45 | The Business (Visual: Clean graphic of the 5% fee and revenue pillars)**

> "We monetize through a simple 5% transaction fee and logistics arbitrage. We aren't asking merchants to change their behavior—we’re just giving them the tools to professionalize it. We’ve built this on a robust Laravel-Redis stack designed to scale to thousands of concurrent orders."

**1:45 - 2:00 | The Close (Visual: Founder on camera or Takeer Logo with contact info)**

> "We are Takeer. We’re closing the trust gap and building the rails for the next generation of African commerce. We’re currently looking for partners to help us dominate the Tanzanian market. Let’s talk."

### **Pro-Tip for Your Recording**

* **Voice:** Use a clear, energetic tone. If you are targeting Tanzanian investors, a "Bongo Tech" vibe—professional but deeply rooted in local context (mentioning Kariakoo or M-Pesa)—is very effective.
* **Video:** Use a tool like **Loom** or **ScreenStudio** to record your screen while you click through the PWA you just built.
