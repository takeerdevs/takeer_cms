
### The Pitch Deck Slide: Monetization & Unit Economics

**[Slide Title:] How Takeer Captures Value**
*(Visual layout: Split the slide into three distinct pillars to show diversified revenue).*

* **Pillar 1: The Trust Take-Rate (Transactional)**
* **The Mechanic:** A flat 5% fee on completed sales.
* **The Value Prop:** Merchants happily pay this because our escrow system and frictionless mobile-money checkout actively increase their conversion rates and eliminate buyer hesitation. We only win when they win.


* **Pillar 2: Last-Mile Arbitrage (Logistical)**
* **The Mechanic:** Capturing the spread between the retail buyer delivery fee and our wholesale fixed-rate terminal boda contracts.
* **The Value Prop:** By charging the buyer a standard TZS 18,000 for a long-distance terminal dispatch, but paying our contracted riders a fixed TZS 15,000, we instantly generate a TZS 3,000 profit per routed package to cover server overhead, with zero vehicle maintenance costs.


* **Pillar 3: Takeer PRO (SaaS MRR)**
* **The Mechanic:** A TZS 25,000/month subscription for high-volume boutiques.
* **The Value Prop:** Unlocks priority API dispatching, zero platform transaction fees, and downloadable customer data for WhatsApp remarketing. This provides the company with predictable Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) outside of seasonal shopping dips.
