This **Technical Pitch Deck** is designed to shift the conversation from "I’m building an app" to "I’m building the future of African Trade." It focuses on how Takeer solves the **Trust Gap** and the **Logistics Chaos** using AI and Escrow.

## **Takeer: The AI-Powered Social Commerce Infrastructure**

*Subtitle: Bridging the Trust Gap in African Digital Trade.*

---

### **Slide 1: The Problem (The "DM for Price" Trap)**

* **The Fragmentation:** Thousands of merchants on Instagram/TikTok, but no way to buy instantly.
* **The Friction:** 80% of sales are lost in the "How much?" and "Is it available?" back-and-forth.
* **The Trust Gap:** Buyers are afraid to pay upfront; Merchants are afraid of "Pay on Delivery" scams.
* **The Chaos:** Logistics (Boda/Buses) are unorganized and untracked.

### **Slide 2: The Solution (Takeer)**

* **One-Click Bio-Link:** A high-performance PWA that turns social media profiles into professional stores.
* **Escrow-as-a-Service:** We hold the money until the buyer verifies the goods.
* **Video-Verified Logistics:** Proof of dispatch and proof of unboxing recorded directly in-app.
* **Conversational AI:** A ChatGPT-like shopping assistant that finds products using natural language.

### **Slide 3: Phase-Based Technology Stack**

* **Core Engine:** Laravel 11 + Inertia.js (Native App speed on the Web).
* **The Trust Chain:** Native Escrow logic + M-Pesa B2C/C2B Integration.
* **AI Vision Layer:** Automated product tagging (Gemini Vision) so merchants never have to type descriptions.
* **Media Engine:** HLS Video Streaming for "TikTok-style" instant-play shoppable posts.

### **Slide 4: The "Safe-Ship" Logistics Model**

* **Intercity (Buses):** Automated waybill OCR tracking and 72-hour escrow release.
* **Local (Boda):** "Doorstep Verification" logic. PIN-release system ensures the Boda is paid and the customer is happy simultaneously.
* **Evidence Vault:** side-by-side video comparison for dispute resolution.

### **Slide 5: Conversational Commerce (The Competitive Edge)**

* **The AI Personal Shopper:** Users find products by chatting ("Find me a black shirt for 30k").
* **Semantic Search:** Using Vector embeddings to find products even if the merchant didn't use the right keywords.
* **Voice-Ready:** Optimized for the "Voice Note" culture of East Africa.

### **Slide 6: Business Model (The 5% Engine)**

* **Revenue:** 5% commission per successful transaction.
* **Merchant Retention:** Wallet & Withdrawal system that simplifies business finances.
* **Scalability:** Takeer doesn't own inventory or bikes; we own the **Data** and the **Trust Protocol**.

### **Slide 7: Roadmap (2026)**

* **Q1: The Kariakoo 10.** Beta launch with 10 high-volume influencers/merchants.
* **Q2: Logistics Expansion.** Full integration with major bus lines (Tashrif, Abood, etc.).
* **Q3: AI Personal Shopper.** Launch of the Natural Language "Chat-to-Buy" interface.
* **Q4: Takeer Pay.** Introducing a merchant credit system based on sales data.
